Insurance and Policies

Phone Calls
Please text/call (410-775-6430) or email (azizobgyn@outlook.com) during regular office hours for all medical needs including appointments, prescriptions and lab results. Please go to labor and delivery if you are in labor or the emergency room during a medical emergency.

Outside of normal business hours, a Doctor is on call for emergencies only.

Appointment Policies
Please note you will be charged $50 fee for 48 hour cancellation/reschedule appointment. We appreciate early notification of your change of plans so that we may offer that appointment to another patient in need. After three missed appointments, the scheduling of future appointments will be at the discretion of the office.

Please bring insurance card, ID, credit card and paper copies of any records you want to discuss with the doctor.

Laboratory Services and Radiology Imaging Services
You can access results here:
Lab Results: https://patient.labcorp.com/landing
Imaging Results: https://www.advancedradiology.com/for-patients/patient-portal

For your convenience, we partnered with Labcorp for on-site lab services and Advanced Radiology for imaging services. However, it is the patient’s responsibility to ensure the workup is obtained and our office receive the results in a timely manner. If we do not receive the results, we cannot review them and provide optimal care.

Billing
We now require a credit card on file; This policy authorizes Aziz Obgyn to charge the credit card on file for any patient-responsible balances for services received after insurance processing. You will continue to receive a letter in the mail from your insurance carrier that explains how much of your office visit they paid and how much you are responsible to pay. This is called an Explanation of Benefits (EOB).

Any billing questions or concerns, please send us an email at azizobgyn@outlook.com with complete details.

Payment Policies
Co-payment is collected from you at the time of service. If the required payment is not made at the time of service. a $10 billing fee will be charged to your account. Payments that do not clear the bank and or are not credited to our account for any reason will be charged a processing fee of $5.00 and a bank fee of $25.00.

Certain procedures, such as IUDs require insurance pre-authorization. Please contact your individual insurance carrier to verify your benefits.

Please feel free to contact our office for fee schedules, concerns, problems or difficulty with payment. We will do our best to come to mutually satisfactory payment plans that will allow us to provide uninterrupted medical care.

Transfer of Care
We are excited to take care of you. Per our policy, please drop off paper copies of your complete medical records to our office Monday - Friday 8 am - 4 pm. Upon review we will give you an appointment within 1 week.

Your Medical Records
Please send email for records request to azizobgyn@outlook.com (include name, date of birth and place of transfer). We will process once we receive your completed form, please note there is 2-3 week processing time and $25 records fee.

Maryland law (Health General Sec. 4-304) allows physicians to charge patients (or the patient's "personal representative") a fee for copying medical records. The charges may be adjusted annually for inflation.

About Us

We offer a full range of Obstetric and Gynecologic Services in a caring and compassionate manner. We provide a complete range of reproductive associated health care for women of all ages, including high and low risk obstetrics, contraception and menopausal care.

We foster an open-minded approach, allowing patients much input into all health care decisions, including management of labor. With open communication with our patients, we hope to establish mutual understanding, respect, confidence and trust.

Dr. Homayara Haque Aziz

Dr. Homayara Haque Aziz is board-certified in Obstetrics and Gynecology and is a fellow of the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology. She provides personalized prenatal care for routine and high-risk pregnancies, delivery, C-Section, and postpartem care.

She performs Hysterectomy, Ovarian Surgery, Fibroid Surgery, Ovarian Cyst removal with minimally invasive options. She also offers Endometrial Ablation for heavy bleeding, Essure/Bilateral Tubal Ligation for permanent sterilization, D&C. If you are experiencing heavy bleeding, pelvic pain, fibroid uterus, ovarian cysts, please come in for evaluation or 2nd opinion.

Dr Aziz is originally from NYC. She volunteers extensively in the community educating women regarding Safe Sex, Contraception and Women’s Health. She is passionate about women’s education and access to healthcare. During her free time, she enjoys Travelling, Baking, Reading, Gardening, and Painting. ​She speaks English, Hindi, Bengali, Urdu, and Arabic.
